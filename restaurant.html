The Restaurant

Madam Lee

Nestled in the quiet town of Ipoh, this cozy little restaurant is a blend of diverse Asian cuisines. What started out as a small food outlet by a petite Madam lee more than 16 years ago (established in 1991) has become one of the most reputable restaurant in Ipoh. Famous for its original receipt, Assam House has attracted many to its simple style of preparing dishes, regardless of local and internation residents.

The Assam House's Way

Assam House undoubtedly possess representative and deep contemplation, fetching savory orthodox Asian Cuisine such as Malaysia Cuisine, Nyonya Cuisine, Indonesia Dishes, Thai Food, Vietnamese Fare and authentic Chinese non Spicy Food to the local and international residents, whereas the traditional Nyonya cooking styles concocted well with the local spices, Assam House joyfully disclosed the unique exquisite food of Asian.

Assam House's Curry Assam Fish Head permeated an Asia flavor, adopting the freshest head of Garoupa fish and stuffed with Citronella, Onion, and Chilli that full of Vitamin A and capable to counteract harmful oxiding substances concurrently.
